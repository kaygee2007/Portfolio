Here's who I am & what I do

I'm Chelsea, a 19-year-old content creator and dancer with a passion that took root when I was just 14. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed a deep understanding of what it takes to engage audiences through digital media. Collaboration is at the heart of my creative process; being an extrovert, I thrive on the energy of working with others. The beauty of creating content with different people lies in the diverse perspectives and skills each person brings to the table. It's a constant learning experience, and I cherish every opportunity to expand my knowledge and creativity.

My father has been a significant influence on my journey. With a career spanning across renowned radio stations like Metro FM, Ukozi FM, and YFM, he instilled in me the fundamentals of social media and content creation from a young age.
